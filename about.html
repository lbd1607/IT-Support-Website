College of Education - Technical Support

About us

The College of Education’s IT Support department provides specialized technical support that falls outside the realm of support that Computer Services provides. From setting up classroom equipment to fixing complex technological issues, IT Support works to keep the College of Education’s daily operations running smoothly.

Mission

IT Support’s mission is to provide excellent service by ensuring that our technology facilitates the success of our faculty, staff, and students in the College of Education.

Values

In addition to the values of Missouri State and the College of Education, IT Support values:

  • Respect
  • Effective communication
  • Honesty
  • Fairness
  • Responsibility and accountability
  • Fortitude

Departments and locations

Although the College of Education can be found in almost every building on campus, our services are headquartered in Hill Hall. Additionally, several departments, including IT Support, are based downtown in Park Central Office Building (PCOB), just off the Square.

The College of Education’s departments include:

  • Child Education and Family Studies
  • Counseling, Leadership, and Special Education
  • Reading, Foundations, and Technology
  • Secondary Education

Contact information

Our office hours are from 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM on weekdays and during the summer when the university is in operation. To receive prompt and effective technical support, please refer to our Documentation or file a Ticket.
